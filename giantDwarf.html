Giant | Dwarf

The familiar "Giant Dwarf" game. The app will provide you all the information you need about your giant. Be creative and think, what will make him happy?

Overview

 

Having decided to do this project for my friends, it is very close to my heart.
Even though Giant Dwarf seems like a simple game, in order to play it you would need
a lot of details about your giant e.g. exact address, preferred tastes and hobbies.
Moreover, managing a big group of players might be difficult,
as you would need to match every player a dwarf randomly.
I realized we needed an app and decided to design and develop it by myself.

#React #Node j.s #MongoDB, have fun :)
