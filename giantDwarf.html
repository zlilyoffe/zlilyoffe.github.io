Giant | Dwarf

The familiar "Giant Dwarf" game. You can sign up, create or join a group and get a random giant. The app will provide you all the information you need about your giant. Be creative and think, what will make him happy?

UX Prototype

Overview

 

Having decided to do this project for my friends, it is very close to my heart. Some of us live in rented apartments, which results in many apartment moves and changes. A desire to play Giant Dwarf encounters a number of challenges - constant synchronization of locations, floors, apartments, access codes, etc. Also, how can we keep complete confidentiality, and how we can all participate, someone needs to make the lottery.
I realized we needed an app and tried to challenge myself by writing code for it.

#React #Node j.s #MongoDB, have fun :)
