About Me

Hello, I'm Zlil Yoffe Simhon,
A former architect aspiring to become a professional UX&UI designer.
I desire to distill what I love and dream about. I want to influence how people live in our world, just as it sounds, and that also embodies an architect's vocation in the real world, as well as the designer in the virtual world.
As a matter of fact, every single day has brought me more knowledge and that is also what makes developing in front of architecture so thrilling. It is a world that is renewed and grows in its capabilities every day.
I love the dynamic nature of programming and being able to watch how everything works, links together, correct, rebuilds, corrects again - everything is dynamic and action-packed.
As a designer, I bring a connection between several worlds, including color, shape, emotion, experience, research, and thinking about the user, as well as practice, knowledge of content, and coding.
A blend of it, these are the foundations that lead to a successful project.
