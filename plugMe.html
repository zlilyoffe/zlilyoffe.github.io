Overview

 

As an environmental amateur, I wanted to ease the lives of electric vehicle drivers everywhere.
EV adoption introduces new challenges like charging duration and navigation to the station.
In order to address these problems, I decided to design an app that helps the driver find the best charging station.

  design process  

Empathize - Define - Ideate - Prototype

Dive in to The problem

Long charging time - Usually the charging power is low, what results hours of charging.
Complex navigation - Station distribution is unexpected making it hard to find them.
Waiting in line - Drivers are not coordinated and might arrive to the same station in the same time.

Experiencing the problem

One of the best ways to explore an existing problem is to go through it by yourself (if possible).
It is important to remember that not everyone responds in the same way to the same challenge,
and therefore it is imperative to establish a diverse user research.
  I decided to rent an electric car and experience driving and charging.

 

Market Research

 

Market players |   PlugShare, Grivo, Energy, EV, Israel EV club

Plugshare

PlugShare has noticeable problems :
First, when you install the app, you need to update your car details, what makes all of your searches based on this.
If you have another car? or if you want to finding a station for a friend?
Second, PlugShare don't allow you to make reservations, pay and know what you have around.

 

Grivo

While Grivo is a high-quality app, communicate design, navigate to the station, help options, and many more!
What you don't have in Grivo is:

- The option to make reservations.
- The full information about what you have around.
- Get a massage when the charging is finished.
- General information about a new station or new regulations.
- A payment method that works.
- The app working just in Israel, we need an international App

The driver needs an app that contains all the options in one place.
Now we can find Facebook groups, WhatsApp groups, and applications like Wallbox That allows managing your current station.

User research

Questionnaires for 27 drivers.
To gather as much measurable information regarding habits, preferences, ways of charging, and the main use methods.
In this part, I interviewed 27 people.
After analyzing the questionnaires, I learned a lot about the way the product should be developed,
what needs to be emphasized and what is further explored in the next stage of in-depth interviews with users.

 

User interview

 

In-depth interviews, the purpose is to understand the results from the quantitative research phase.
At this point, I focused on understanding behavior patterns in specific situations, preferences, and complex ways of thinking.

Personas

 

Personas are descriptive figures, built on the data collected from the study.
Personas represent the different types of users who will use the product, application, site, or service offered in a similar way.
These can be quantified, researched and relevant lessons and thoughts for further planning and design can be derived from them. The personas will help the designer understand the needs, experiences, goals, and behavior of the users.

 

The solution

 
EV drivers App
 

A simple interface, that will help you to find and reach the relevant station next to you.
Focused filtering of characteristics that are important to you.

The user will be able to see photos, details, if the station is occupied (and for how long),
and other drivers' scores which will verify the quality, rapidness of charging, prices, and description of the station and what you have next to it.

The user will be able to share stations he liked or discovered, get a higher rating, and be exposed to new stations.

The user can update online, on how much time he has left to be on this station.

User flow

 

User flow is a schematic flow chart that a typical user passes in order to complete a task.
User flow takes the user from the entry point through actions, to the desired result and the final action.
In my case, I chose a User flow for a new user on his way to find the ideal station.
This is one of many different types of User flows.
